Artificial Intelligence

Skribbles Classification, 2020

Kaggle Competition for Image Classificiation. Image were 100x100px skribbles with noise.
Classification was made between 30 different classes.
Classification has been made using computer vision methods : denoising, data augmentation, choice of model (CNN in our case), hyperparameter tuning, testing.

Email Classification, 2020

Kaggle Competition for classification of hishing emails based on different extracted features of an email.
Usage of Deep Learning as well as various classic Models of classification (Random Forest, Decision Tree, kNN).

Chess AI, 2018

For research project, contribution to an Artificial Intelligence for Chess. Work on evaluation of the value of different pieces in order to get better sense of next move's efficiency.

Rush Hour AI, 2019

Implementing an Artificial Intelligence for the game Rush Hour (puzzle game, where the Red Car has to get out but is stuck by other cars). Implementing the best A* algorithm possible.
